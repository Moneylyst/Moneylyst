Review:
Sweatcoin is a fitness app that rewards users for their physical activity by giving them a virtual currency called sweatcoins which can be exchanged for products and services. The app uses your phone's accelerometer and GPS to track your movement, and converts your steps into sweatcoins. The app is a fun and engaging way to incentivize physical activity and earn rewards.

3. Evidation

Review:
Evidation is a free fitness app that rewards users for tracking their health and fitness progress. Users earn points that they can redeem for cash, gift cards, or donate to charity. The app tracks your activity, such as walking, running, swimming, and more, and rewards up to 80 points per activity each day. Users have to collect 10,000 points to withdraw $10.
